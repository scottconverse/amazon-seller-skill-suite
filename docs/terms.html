Unlike standalone prompts, these skills run autonomously in Claude's CoWork mode. By using them, you acknowledge additional risks:

6.1 Autonomous Activation

Skills activate automatically based on Claude's interpretation of your queries. A skill may activate when you do not expect it, provide advice outside its intended domain, or chain recommendations across multiple skills without explicit confirmation at each step.

Skills pass data to each other through a handoff protocol. Errors, misinterpretations, or hallucinations in one skill's output may cascade to downstream skills, compounding inaccuracies.
